Takaku tobu

In Japenese means "fly high". It's a reminder that no dream is too big, no mountain is too high. And that I may not make it immediately -- but absolutely and definitely!

Vision

A home where nature and technology co-exist; where productivity, creativity and continouos learing is a lifestyle; and a place where laughter and music is in harmony.

Mission

To be a venue for family and friends to bond. To be the go-to place for year round celebrations. To be a one-stop-shop to get things done and/or make things fun. And when needed, a sanctuary for a lost and tired soul to re-energize.

...cause even the strongest flyer need a place and time to rest.
-Ray Tan, 2015
